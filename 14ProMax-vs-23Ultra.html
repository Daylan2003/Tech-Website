Apple's IPhone 14 Pro Max vs Samsung's S23 Ultra

Created by: D.Maharaj

About the Author

Hi ! I am a Computer Engineering Student and tech enthusiast. I made this website to give you accurate and up to date information on the latest tech to help you find which tech device is right for you and so you can make a choice yourself.

I am not going to biased towards any brand but a full disclosure, my top choice for a phone is the Google Pixel 7 Pro and my top choice in a tablet is the Samsung Galaxy tab S9 Ultra.

Battery Life

The new IPhone 14 Pro Max comes with a 4,323 mAh battery. It should last for around 9.5 hours of on-screen time during a regular day. On the other hand, the S23 Ultra comes with a 5,000 mAh battery. Despite having a larger battery capacity, it should last upwards of 8 hours of on-screen time during a regular day of usage. Both are more than enough to last an average user during the entire day without a charge, however, keep in mind this time will decrease as the battery degrades and external factors such as temperature will affect the battery life.

Display

The S23 Ultra comes with a Dynamic AMOLED 2X screen, a 120HZ display, HDR10+, 1200 nits in (HBM) and 1750 nits at the peak brightness.

The 14 Pro Max comes with a LTPO Super Retina XDR OLED, a 120Hz display, HDR10, Dolby Vision, 1000 nits (typ) and 2000 nits in (HBM).

The S23 Ultra Features a 17.3 cm screen with a resolution of 1440 x 3088 pixels or a 500 ppi density. Whereas, the 14 Pro Max has a slightly smaller 17 cm screen with a resolution 1290 x 2796 pixels or a 460 ppi density. While on paper, the resolution of the IPhone is less than that of the Samsung, to the human eye, you will not notice the difference.
